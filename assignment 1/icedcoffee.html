Iced Coffee

How is Iced Coffee made?

Iced coffee is made through a variety of different processes. Some techniques are, Aeropress, chilled drip coffee, overnight steeping, or the Japanese pour over.

Recipes to try at home

Horchata Iced Coffee

  1. Prepare some iced coffee using your favorite method, or one of the methods mentioned above.
  2. Then prepare some horchata using your powdered mix of choice.
  3. In a glass, fill with ice about half way up, and pour iced coffee till glass is 3/4th full.
  4. Top of the remainder with some horchata, and you’ve got a perfectly balanced cinnamon-y iced coffee!

Current favorite on the go:

Starbucks Pumpkin Creme Cold Brew:
This drink is shamelessly my go-to drink during the fall. I’m a sucker for anything pumpkin, and this one is deliciously that without being over powering. It is a nice balance of sweet and tart, as the only sugar added is through the cold foam. I 10/10 recommend any coffee and pumpkin lovers to try this one out!
